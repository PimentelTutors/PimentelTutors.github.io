Section I: Key Takeaways
Letters of Recommendation

“A man's real life is that accorded to him in the thoughts of other men by reason of respect...”
Joseph Conrad
When required, letters of recommendation can really help a student's application. However, knowing how to request and obtain them can sometimes be tricky. A good way for students to start identifying potential recommenders is by thinking about people with whom they have established rapport. Potentially, these will be the individuals who support them as they seek letters of recommendation.
Many colleges require letters of recommendation—whether from teachers or other adults who know students and applicants well. Choosing people who can speak to one’s academic abilities, character, and work ethic is essential.
